# NoFvce Crew — Branding

## Concept

NoFvce Crew is not just a car club.

The project represents:
- automotive culture
- underground lifestyle
- exclusivity
- identity
- community

The idea is to create a premium and minimalist collective,
focused on aesthetics, presence and belonging.

---

# Meaning of the Name

"NoFvce" represents anonymity and identity at the same time.

The name creates the feeling of:
- mystery
- underground culture
- collective mentality
- lifestyle movement

It should feel more like a movement or brand than a traditional car club.
